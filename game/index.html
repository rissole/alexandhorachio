The wonky tale of Alex and Horachio the talking kazoo has finally made it to PC gaming! Explore a Final Fantasy and Undertale-inspired JRPG, and master the unique, rhythm game style battle system.

Features

  • A real life JRPG where you play as Alex, the Good Boy.
  • Create your own attacks to the beat with the rhythm game-inspired battle system.
  • It's funny! Play it many times to get all the dialogue! Please
  • Rich four dimensional demon instrument and Blakeopolis lore!
  • A little bit wonky (buggy probably)
